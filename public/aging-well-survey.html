Six PERMA+ pillars

The areas this survey reflects on

Each pillar is a different lens on the same life. None matters more than another — together they map how well you're flourishing right now.

P

Positive Emotions

The everyday experience of joy, contentment, gratitude, hope — the warm side of life as it actually shows up for you.

E

Engagement

Being so absorbed in what you're doing that time bends — the experience of flow, focus, and finding yourself fully here.

R

Relationships

Belonging, support, and feeling genuinely connected to the people who shape your life — close ties and wider community alike.

M

Meaning

The sense of purpose and direction that makes the days add up — what your life is for, and what your contributions mean.

A

Accomplishment

Growth, capability, and momentum — the ongoing experience of mastering, completing, and quietly becoming more of who you are.

H

Health

Sleep, movement, energy — the physical foundation that everything else rests on. Practising what your body actually needs.
