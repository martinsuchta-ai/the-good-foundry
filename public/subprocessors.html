Sub-Processors engaged in delivering Wellbeing Matters @ Work

This page is the canonical, version-pinned list of every third-party we engage to deliver the platform. It is cited as Schedule A of our Data Processing Agreement and is the single source of truth for "who else touches your data".

Any change to the list — adding a new sub-processor, retiring one, or changing a region — is recorded in the change log at the bottom of this page with a new version stamp.

02 · Optional add-on scope

Engaged only when an add-on is contracted.

Any sub-processor underpinning an optional contracted add-on (e.g. AI Seeding, Personalised Coaching Videos, NotebookLM coaching summaries) is not in the default scope. It is engaged only when that add-on is explicitly contracted, with the third party named and the data flow documented in writing under the contract.

Default posture: a client deployment carries NONE of these sub-processors unless the corresponding add-on appears on the Statement of Service. If you've contracted only the base measurement platform, the Default Scope above is the complete sub-processor list for your deployment.

03 · Bindings & guarantees

How each engagement is bound.

  • Confidentiality + DPA on every engagement — without exception. The standard binding is the sub-processor's own DPA + a TGF-side mirror addendum.
  • No marketing / analytics / AI training reuse — client data is never reused outside delivering the contracted service.
  • 72-hour breach notification from any sub-processor flows through to you within the same 72-hour window (Australian Privacy Act NDB scheme; GDPR Article 33 equivalent for EU clients).
  • Sub-processor deletion on contract termination — within 35 days of a written deletion request, your data is removed from every listed location, certified by TGF + each sub-processor.
  • Notice of changes — material sub-processor changes (new entrant, region change, retirement) are notified to all active clients in writing at least 30 days before activation, with the right to object.
