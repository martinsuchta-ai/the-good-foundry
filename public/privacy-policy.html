What Information Do We Collect?

We collect information about you and your use of our Services from a variety of sources. The information we collect depends on how you use our Services — whether as an Administrator, Respondent, or Visitor.

(a) Information We Generally Collect

  • Usage information. Data about how you interact with our Services, including pages visited, clicks, and activity timing.
  • Device and browser data. IP address, operating system, browser details, and time of visit — which may also indicate your location.
  • Log data. Log files recording each server access, including originating IP address, to maintain an audit trail and improve our Services.
  • Referral data. Information about the source that referred you to our websites.
  • Other submitted data. Personal information provided in testimonials, events, downloads, purchases, or social media interactions.
(b) Information Specific to Administrators & Respondents

(c) Information Specific to Administrators

  • Survey data. Survey questions and related information created and launched through the Services.
  • Billing details. Cardholder name, billing address, email, card number, expiry date, and security code (collected by our card processor).
  • Account settings. Name, email, default language, time zone, and other preferences on your account or profile page.

13

Google User Data

When you choose to connect Google Fit to your Wellbeing Vault, Wellbeing Matters @ Work requests permission to read the following data from your Google account:

  • Activity data — daily step counts, active minutes, distance covered (fitness.activity.read)
  • Body composition — weight and BMI (fitness.body.read)
  • Heart rate — daily average resting heart rate (fitness.heart_rate.read)
  • Sleep — nightly sleep duration and stages (fitness.sleep.read)

How we use this data

  • To populate your personal wellness trend charts in your Wellbeing Vault, correlating objective data with your self-reported PERMA+H evaluation scores.
  • To generate aggregated, anonymised cohort averages (groups of 10+ respondents) for organisational reporting — with no individual identifiability.

How we do not use this data

  • We do not sell your Google Fit data to any third party.
  • We do not share your Google Fit data with your employer in identifiable form.
  • We do not use it for advertising.
  • We do not use it to make decisions about your employment.

Storage and retention

  • Data is stored encrypted at rest on our SiteGround infrastructure (Sydney, Australia region).
  • Daily aggregates are retained while your account is active; raw event-level data is not retained.
  • On disconnection from your Wellbeing Vault settings, all stored Google Fit data is deleted within 30 days.
  • On account closure, all data is deleted within 30 days.

Your control

  • You can disconnect Google Fit at any time from Vault → Settings → Integrations.
  • You can revoke our access from your Google Account → Security → Third-party apps.
  • You can request deletion of all stored data by emailing privacy@permahsurvey.com.

Compliance

Wellbeing Matters @ Work's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs to any other app will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
