Six dimensions of LiveWell

The areas this survey reflects on

Each dimension is a different lens on the same life. None is more important than another — together they map the shape of how you're ageing well right now.

Building Emotional Resilience

Your ability to navigate stressful moments with hope, confidence, and acceptance — meeting hard things without being undone by them.

Cultivating Inclusion & Belonging

Feeling valued, accepted, and genuinely connected in the relationships and communities you're part of.

Knowing Me

Reflecting on your strengths through past experience, present commitments, and the future you're moving toward.

Learning & Growing

The ongoing experience of personal evolution and accomplishment — the sense that you're still becoming more of who you are.

Maintaining Purpose

A felt sense of significance — knowing what your life is about, and what your most meaningful contributions are.

Optimizing Health

Practising what your body, mind, and spirit actually need — the foundation everything else stands on.
