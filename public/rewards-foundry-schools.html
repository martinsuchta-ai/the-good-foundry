Rewards Foundry for Schools

A simple, fair way to reward positive behaviour and discourage negative behaviour across Me · We · Us. Scan, name the moment, and the wallet updates. A Power-Up that bolts onto any active Wellbeing Matters @ School subscription.

Inspired by PERMA(H)
Two directions

Spin Up. Spin Down.

Every behaviour pulls the wallet in a direction. Spin Up adds points for things worth recognising; Spin Down subtracts points for things worth pausing on. Same simple scan, opposite sign.

Spin Up — recognise.

A teacher, aide, or peer-mentor scans a Spin Up code when a student shows up well: kindness to a classmate, focused effort, looking out for someone, owning a mistake, bringing curiosity. Points land in the wallet automatically.

Points + added to the wallet

Spin Down — reflect.

A trusted person scans a Spin Down code when a behaviour misses the mark: disruption, unkindness, repeated lateness, off-task patterns. Points come out of the wallet — with the message that the score is recoverable on the next round.

Points − subtracted from the wallet
Three levels

Me · We · Us.

Behaviour shows up at three levels — what a student does alone, what they bring to their class, and how they contribute to the whole school. Spin tokens are organised the same way, with point bands scaled to match.

Me

The Student

Personal practice — showing up on time, completing work, looking after self. Default point band: ±5.

We

The Class

Class contribution — helping a peer, contributing to group work, running a small initiative. Default point band: ±10.

Us

The School

Whole-school contribution — representing the school, looking after grounds, supporting a community moment. Default point band: ±20.

How a spin happens

Four taps. No paperwork.

Designed to slot into the day without slowing it down. Teachers carry a single picker, or scan a code on a card or classroom poster.

1
Scan QR
Open the camera, scan the behaviour’s QR code (on a card, poster, or printed sheet). Resolves to a Spin Up or Spin Down.
2
Enter code or email
Type the student’s access KEY (or email). Same identification mechanism the rest of Wellbeing Matters uses.
3
Confirm spin
Review the behaviour, the direction, and the points. One tap to confirm.
4
Wallet updates
Points land in the student’s wallet immediately. They can see why — the behaviour title, the date, and who recognised it.
Your full dashboard, included

See every wallet movement.

The Schools Power-Up comes with the same drill-down dashboard the rest of Wellbeing Matters has, organised by the Me · We · Us model.

Me view

Per-student wallet history.

Every Spin Up + Spin Down on this student’s wallet, organised by date and by PERMA-H dimension. Patterns over time become visible at a glance.

We view

Per-class behaviour signal.

How a class is trending — volume of Spin Up vs Spin Down, distribution across the PERMA-H dimensions, top recognised behaviours, top reflected behaviours.

Us view

Whole-school heatmap.

Cross-class comparison + month-over-month trend on the dimensions the school is choosing to amplify. Privacy-clipped: no individual student data surfaces below the n ≥ 5 floor.

Why these categories? Behaviour categories are inspired by PERMA(H) — Positive Emotions, Engagement, Relationships, Meaning, Accomplishment, and Health. The same evidence base the rest of Wellbeing Matters uses, applied to the rhythm of a school day.
